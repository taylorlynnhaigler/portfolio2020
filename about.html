A little about me...

I'm a designer and a developer.

I’m passionate about visual design and am a true believer in creating a balance between beauty and practicality.

I specialize in ux research, testing, brand development, marketing strategy and designing and developing websites.

I’ve been interested in studying people and culture since I was little and poured over my National Geographic collection. My interest evolved into studying art history and archaeology.

I enjoy studying human behavior and art and working to understand how design has influenced civilizations and socities through time and how good design can positively impact our lives.

The desire to create meaningful online experiences that enhance our connection to the world and people around us is what drew me to UX initially. When I saw how I could combine my interest in culture, design and technology to improve our online experiences, I was truly in love.
